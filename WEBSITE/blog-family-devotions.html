Family Devotions for Kids: 5 Simple Ideas to Make Bible Time Fun

JV Jandre van der Walt 📅 July 8, 2026 ⏱ 6 min read
📖
On this page
    On this page

      The fastest way to make family devotions fun is to stop treating them like a lecture and start treating them like a game. Below are 5 simple, low-prep ideas—each takes 10 minutes or less—that turn Bible time into something kids ask for instead of avoid. Pair any of these with a free take-home devotional and you're set for the week.

      🔑 Key Takeaways

      • ✅ Short and consistent beats long and occasional—aim for 5-10 minutes, several times a week.
      • ✅ Kids remember what they act out or repeat far more than what they just hear once.
      • ✅ Let your kids lead sometimes—ownership builds lasting engagement.
