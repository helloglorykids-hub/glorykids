FAQ

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about Glory Kids Membership.

Can I cancel my membership at any time?
+

Absolutely! There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. You can cancel your Glory Kids Membership at any time through your account settings, and you'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

What age groups is the content designed for?
+

Our curriculum covers all children's ages from birth through 12 years old. Lessons are clearly labeled by age group: Nursery (0–2), Toddler (2–4), Preschool (3–5), Early Elementary (5–8), and Upper Elementary (8–12).

Is the content theologically sound?
+

Yes! All of our curriculum is grounded in Scripture and written from a Biblical, evangelical Christian worldview. Our content has been reviewed by pastors and theologians to ensure theological accuracy and faithfulness to God's Word.

Can I use this for my church and at home?
+

One Glory Kids membership can be used within a single church or household. For churches wanting to give access to multiple staff members or volunteers, please contact us for our multi-seat pricing options.

What format are the materials in?
+

Lessons are available as beautifully designed PDFs that you can download and print. Many also include editable versions so you can customize them for your context. Videos are streamed online through your member dashboard.

Is there a free trial?
+

We offer 50+ completely free Bible lessons with no credit card required — perfect for trying our content quality. For full membership access, you can sign up for monthly billing and cancel within 14 days for a full refund if you're not completely satisfied.
