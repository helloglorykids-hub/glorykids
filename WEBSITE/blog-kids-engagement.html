How to Keep Kids Engaged in Church: 7 Proven Strategies That Actually Work

JV Jandre van der Walt 📅 July 8, 2026 ⏱ 8 min read
🌟
On this page
    On this page

      Kids check out in church for one main reason: they feel talked at, not talked with. The fix isn't louder music or flashier videos—it's a handful of proven, low-tech strategies that make kids active participants instead of passive spectators. Below are the 7 that consistently move the needle, drawn from what actually works in Glory Kids classrooms every week.

      🔑 Key Takeaways

      • ✅ Engagement comes from participation, not production value—questions, movement, and object lessons beat louder presentations.
      • ✅ Give every kid a job each week; passive kids disengage, kids with a role stay locked in.
      • ✅ Always close with one specific, doable action—not a vague takeaway.
