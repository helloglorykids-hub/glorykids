More Than Lessons — Spiritual Formation That Sticks

Every Glory Kids lesson is built on three pillars that help children know God, hear His voice, and live out their faith with conviction.

🕊️

VOICE: Seeing and Hearing Jesus

We teach kids how to recognize God's voice through Scripture, prayer, and the Holy Spirit. Children learn that God speaks and that they can grow in listening and responding to Him. This isn't head knowledge — it's personal encounter.

⚡

POWER: Living the Jesus Way

Kids learn how to live out their faith with boldness. Through prayer, faith, and real-life application, children discover how Jesus empowers them to walk in love, courage, and obedience. Faith becomes action, not theory.

🔥

PRESENCE: Marked by His Presence

We help children discover who they are in Christ and how to live from God's presence every day. Kids learn that they are loved, chosen, and never alone. This is the foundation of everything else.
