Quick Answers

Common Questions

How do I access my membership?
After signing up for Glory Kids Membership, you'll receive an email with your login details. You can access all content from your member dashboard immediately.
Can I get a refund?
Yes! We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all new memberships. If you're not completely satisfied, contact us within 14 days for a full refund — no questions asked.
Do you offer group or church discounts?
Yes! For churches wanting multiple seats for their team, we offer special pricing. Get in touch using the contact form and we'll put together a custom quote for you.
Can I request custom curriculum?
Absolutely! We create custom curriculum packs for churches. Fill in the contact form above, select "Custom Content," and tell us about your vision. We'd love to help.
Are the lessons suitable for small churches?
Perfectly suited! Our lessons are designed to work with any size team — even a single volunteer. Everything is clearly scripted and ready to use right out of the box.
Can I use the content in multiple campuses?
One membership covers one ministry location. For multi-site churches, please contact us for our multi-campus licensing options — we make it affordable at scale.
